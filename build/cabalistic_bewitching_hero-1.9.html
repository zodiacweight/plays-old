Author's words

Helen tells Beatrix in particular.

Beatrix

I am not sure I need such game. I can not change my size in this embodiment.

Helen

But I read about extra-decomposers, the species you belong to. It was written extra-decomposers can do that. Why you think you can not?

I do not know this, too. I am rather sure I must leave you soon.

Helen

Beatrix

Sorry, Helen, but I can praise how I live in your aquarium in no way. It can seem like whims, but these are not whims really.

Helen

I understand you why you want to leave this place and why it is not suitable haven for you. There are 2 facts about you such as your world vision and your destination in this world. I know the space in my tank is not so far-flung. Maybe there is no place enough far-flung for you, because you have insatiable fervor to see the world. Besides the fact that the aquarium is a confined space, also the animals are in confined quantity in it. So it is utterly unsuitable haven for you. It may be applicable only as a temporary habitat. An ocean where there is huge plethora of different beings is another thing. Other fact is that you are a scavenger and you have the mission which is an inalienable part of your life. In the same time life in my tank and performing your mission are 2 irreconcilable things. Anything of this is understandable for me. Of course I will part with you when you want.

Beatrix

Thank you, Helen, for your concern to me! I think I will miss you to a certain degree, but my nature compells me to abandon you though I do not want you to feel dejection about my leaving you.

Helen

The reasons of it are irrefutable for me. You have already explained me, Christian has, too. I know he is a human who deals with extra-decomposers in his everyday life. But I am interested in the question about growing your size and your possibility to take part with us in the game in our holidays. I am not sure we can repeal this idea right now by not talking with Christain.

Sorry, Helen. I am going to rebuff it. It refers to my mission again.

Helen, I want you to understand me correctly. I do not want foster resentment in you or incense you. I do not have intentions to impose dejection on you. The idea is that it is my commitment to attend in common world. I can not afford to stay on your aquarium too long. The enormous superflous pollution is the fact which is most important now. There is immense raft of carrion poisoning nature availability of which can lead to catastophic aftermaths which I must confront as you know. There are many other decomposers who is performing this mission, but I must take part in it, too. I must go and fullfill my mission. It is not desirable to postpone this. I do not have it right now, but I do not have to continue life in your aquarium too long.

Now I understand you. I understand it is a real favour that you have aptitudes to sway to our contaminated world. Of course, I can not confront to realization of your intentions. With all of these facts I am releasing you. It must be you will have to exert fulfilling your mission.

Beatrix

Of course I would not.

Helen

When are you going to return to nature?

In 9 days. May we part before I will gone from the house?

Yes, you may.

Good bye.
