Tanita, Luisa and Esteban

What’s up?

Dear friends! I have to tell you something!

Tanita

Somebody got sick?

Joshua

I have made an extremely important decision!

Tanita

So are everybody alive and OK?

Joshua

(seem isn’t hearing her) I gonna Europa!

That’s cool, dude! I wanna too!

It’s not a country. I gonna Europa which is a planet, a Jovian’s satellite.

What the fuck?! (WTF)

Several weeks ago, I’ve got the email from one my acquaintance, Beatriz.

Who is it?

Joshua

She is a peculiar creature on her way, being similar simultaneously to the mermaid and to the human. We have met during my expedition to the Loch-Ness lake where I had been implementing scientific discoveries.

Author's words

Tanita & Luisa exchange meaningful glances.

Esteban

(with a low voice, leaning toward him) And how she is? ─ Cute? Was there something between you?

Joshua

(still not paying attention to anything) So, she has written to me, that she got an offer in order to take a part of the fly to Europa, because she possesses some unique features allowed her to make discoveries under water in that local ocean without any special equipment.But she needs an assistant, some who is a scientist, working in the learning underwater world area.And she recommends me! Everybody keep quiet a while.Then Tanita interrupts a silence.

Tanita

Look, isn’t it dangerous?

(exiting) But what is this fucking Europa for you at all ? It is far away from us ?

Europa is located in 6500 000 km from Jupiter approximately. Please, notice, that this distance is bigger than such one from Earth to Moon. Such a stuff.

Luisa

And what you have forgotten there then?

Esteban

Well, dude, what’s the point indeed? How much they are going to pay?

Joshua

I dunno, I have never be interested. The point is not about money at all.

Tanita

Yep. You just wanna be famous. Of course, the first man who has visited a potentially habitable planet.

Joshua

(slightly amazed) So are you aware?

Tanita

I am. I know that Europa is the expectant number one to be named a second planet in our Solar system having life.

Joshua

Exactly! It is covered with a layer of ice with a very deep ocean wrapping all planet beneath it. Scientists believe that its depth may reach 100 km. So, if there the liquid water is established it is quite possible that the life may be found also. Just imagine ─ extraterrestrial life!

Girl

Well, yep, that’s cool. Like spaceships ply endless universe and everything like this. Congratulations, dude!

Tanita

(ironically) Yeah!

Luisa

But whether it was impossible to send somebody but you? I’m very worried for you. For sure it is very dangerous!

Joshua

Well, that is! Space flights are always associated with danger. But that is worth of risk. Even if I never back, I will regret of nothing.

Tanita

(ironically again, citing the immortal mem from the Diamond Arm movie) Maybe I even be rewarded… posthumously!

In half of year.

Yes, yet another crew member is Cristian. He is acquaintance of Beatriz also, he is wunderkind. He is a lad, but his mind and behavior are arranged absolutely like such of the adult person. But he won’t get out of the ship, his duty is to oversee us via appliances.

I and Beatriz are going to go underwater.

Esteban

(admiringly) Wow, dude!Cool!

What temperature is there?

Above zero on Celsius. The water is liquid, as I have noticed. That’s the point.

You, it seems, said that all the ocean is covered with ice?

Yes, it is.

In many spots, yes. But somewhere not exactly. We will get a special equipment allowing us to drill a hole through the ice and slide down.

So, you say this is near Jupiter?

Precisely!

But this is so far away! as far as I know, Mars is much closer to Earth, but even there it’s very cold. Is this true?

Exactly. Jupiter is approximately 15 times farther away from Earth than Mars (~780 000 000/55 000 000)

Luisa

But then it must be very cold there. And how at Europa can be liquid water? Or has it, like Earth, a hot core?

Joshua

No, Europa is far smaller than the Earth. And it is even smaller than Mars. It is almost the same size as Moon.

Luisa

So, where does the liquid water come from?

Joshua

Yes, this is an interesting question. There are several hypotheses. One which is the most trustworthy is that Europa is warmed because of the gravitational field of Jupiter. During the process of rotation around him it either stretches toward him or it shrinks. This inner tension also leads to the production of warmth. At least it is exactly known that under the ice layer there is a liquid and very deep ocean!

(also thoughtfully) Funny…

And?!

Did they call?

Most probably
