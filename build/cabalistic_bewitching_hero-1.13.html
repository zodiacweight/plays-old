Christian gets Beatrix's proposal

Helen

Hi, Christian.

Christian

Hi, girls.

Judy & Cassandra & Beatrix

Hi, Christian.

Helen

We are going to tell you a news. Beatrix has advice for you. It is related to your inquiry . I think you have some dearth of your knowledges about extra-decomposers. The proposal is about something what can help you to fill it.

Christian

Really?

Helen

I would not like kid with you. But the trust is that you will must speak with one devil creature if you acceptthe offer. This devil has created extra-decomposers and can tell you very much about their Beatrix posits that it is not somebody unrelenting to humans.

Beatrix

Yes, Christian. Be calm, please. I want tell you about this creature. I think the idea to go is very good idea.

Christian

But devil is associated with evil for people.

Beatrix

It is just preconceptions. This woman is not evil for us. She is very good-looking and friendly person. She helped me to recover after my illness which I had gotten afterward of one unpleasant story occured in my life. Nobody would help me except her. I am so thankful to her!

Helen

But you said she is very hard with you. You have liabilities of her. You are not free and so you do not like your life with her.

Beatrix

Yes, when I was free it was better. Now I live in the lake with her and there I am urged to serve her. But I am very thankful to the woman-devil now. Maybe I would not live now if she was not in my life. I feel I owe her despite it incites me to rack my brains.

Judy

What responsibility do you have of her?

Beatrix

I have many responsibilities of her. I have role of a maidservant, nurse and toy for little hatched cubs of marine inhabitants. Those cubs are not her descendants, but she is a ringleader of this underwater world. She can assign me such roles which she thinks I must comply. So I help her to arrange her places and refuges under water which she uses with different purposes. I am used as a nurse for cubs of little animals and as a toy for cubs of big animals such as cats. They play with me like cats with cordage what I don't like very much.

Judy

Do you want to say that cats live in the lake?

Beatrix

No, I don't say this. Cats don't live in the lake, but sometimes when I am out of the lake they meet me and play with me.

Cassandra

Then you have to be out of the lake not very often and when you are out you have to fly in such high where the cats cannot reach you.

Beatrix

Yes, you are right.

Cassandra

Does the host of the court know about your life in the lake?

Beatrix

No, he does not.

Cassandra

Does he know something about some of animals living in this lake?

Beatrix

I do not know does he know or not.

Christian

Excuse me, please. May I ask to tell me about the lake more. I understand, it is a lake in a somebody's court?

Beatrix

Yes, there is a house and a court of 2 people. There is a lake in the court where I live now. Also there is world under water in this lake with huge plurality of different marine animals. I am one of them.

Christian

How were you gotten to this world?

Beatrix

After my life in Helen's aquarium I was caught by one man who brought me to a shop where I was bought by one family. I lived in their aqarium. They did not feed me how they were must and as consequences I got illness. People decided that it would be too difficult to them to treat me and they throw me out. Later this woman-devil found and cured me near a river. After that she brought me to this court and I started to live with her.

Helen

All right. Let's return to our conversation about your idea to invite Christian for talking with the woman-devil.

Beatrix

What does else interest us about her?

Cassandra

We would like be assured that there be nothing menacing for us.

Beatrix

I have already tried to explain that the woman-devil is not grim for you despite you associate devils with evil. I have no idea how to convince you in other way. More by token the woman-devil must not know that you are a scientist and you want to talk with her in such purposes as bring something new in your learning extra-decomposers. Besides the hosts of the estate who know nothing about wonders in this underwater world must know nothing about your visit in this lake. You will have to mine a diving suit, penetrate into the lake and talk with the woman-devil in there. You can use a big, strong and clever swan to fly over the fence to the lake. After that you can dive into water and meet the woman-devil.

Christian

Can I listen your narrative about the woman-devil as notably as you can tell?

Beatrix

She is like a human in outline. She has 2 hands and legs, head, hair and figure like a human one. But she has some differences from a human. She has big clutches, horns and tail like a lion's. Also she has 3 eyes, 2 of which are on the face like a human and third big one on her polly.

Helen

How do you know this? Did you saw how she changed her clothes?

Beatrix

She is almost always unclad.

Judy

Almost always unclad!?

Cassandra

Are you serious? It is terrible! It is so indecently!

Beatrix

What indecent do you find in it?

Cassandra

It is very indecent among us, people. Did you know about that? How this woman-devil precenses among people?

Beatrix

She is not among people. She lives in the lake among marine animals. It is not indecent in the underwater world.

Judy

Does the woman-devil have wigs to move in water and air like you?

Beatrix

I have never seen she had wigs, but I know she can change imaginary.

Christian

Where she is from and how this information about the woman-devil is connected with extra-decomposers?

Beatrix

She has flew from some place, but I don't know exactly from which one. She has created the extra-decomposers with her cryptic forces.

Christian

I have insatiable desire to engage science very often, but I do not think devil ordinances must be included in my science.

Beatrix

Why?

Christian

Because the science as engagement of a scientist is one activity area and this devil magic is entirely other area.

Beatrix

But I am not agree with this.

Christian

Thank you very much for your proposal, but I want you understand me. You are just one of the specific wonderful creatures in nature. I am a professional scientist. I know about science and job of a scientist tangibly more than you. I know what ideas I must admit and what ideas I must not. Please, don't take offense at me. I do not need information about provenance of extra-decomposers if it is based on magic. Learning their anatomy, lifestyle, roles executed by them and their place in nature - this is what is actual for scientists like me.

Cassandra

I knew that you, Beatrix, would have to induce to persuade Christian to go.

Judy

But I want go with you.

Beatrix

No problems if you have a possibility to mine a diving suit. You can sit on a big swan and fly to the lake. You must do it not from the side of the house. The swan will brought you right. So you don't have to worry.

Judy

But I have no possibility to mine a diving suit. Can I talk with the woman-devil out of the lake?

Beatrix

I will have to negotiate with her about it. Do you see, there is necessaty to hide from eyes of the hosts. So you will have to talk somewhere in the court where the hosts will not see you. Somewhere near the lake behind the trees.
